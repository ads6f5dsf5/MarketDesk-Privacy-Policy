Privacy Policy for MarketDesk

Last updated: 12 December 2025

MarketDesk is a Telegram mini-app that provides market data and alert tools for crypto and stock traders (“the Service”).

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: satto.stream@proton.me

1. Who we are

MarketDesk is a Telegram mini-app (“Mini App”) designed for active traders to view market data and configure alerts for cryptocurrencies and stocks.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at:
Email: satto.stream@proton.me

2. Data we collect

When you use MarketDesk, we may process the following types of data:

2.1 Telegram user information

  • Your Telegram user ID
  • Your Telegram username and display name (if available)

2.2 App usage and preferences

  • Coins, tickers and markets you view
  • Items you add to My Watchlist and My Signals
  • Alert settings (e.g. price levels, funding thresholds, volatility conditions)
  • Basic technical information such as timestamps, language and Telegram client type

We do not collect your phone number, contact list or chat content from Telegram.

3. How we use your data

We use the data described above for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core Service (creating, updating and triggering alerts)
  • To save and load your watchlist, signals and other in-app preferences
  • To improve the performance, reliability and usability of MarketDesk
  • To prevent abuse and protect the Service from spam, misuse or attacks

We do not use your data for personalised advertising or user profiling for marketing purposes.

4. Legal basis (EU/EEA users)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, we process your personal data based on:

  • Performance of a contract – to provide you with the Service you requested when you start using MarketDesk;
  • Legitimate interests – to maintain, secure and improve the Service and to prevent fraud and abuse.

5. Data sharing and third parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

We may share limited data with service providers that help us run MarketDesk, such as:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers (e.g. servers, databases)
  • Error monitoring, logging or analytics tools

These providers only process your data on our behalf and according to our instructions, and are not allowed to use your data for their own marketing purposes.

We may also disclose data if required to do so by law, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or lawful requests;
  • Protect our rights, property or safety, or that of our users or others.

6. Data storage and retention

We store your data only as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

If you stop using MarketDesk for an extended period, we may delete or anonymise your data as part of regular clean-up and maintenance.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights in relation to your personal data, including the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal limitations;
  • Object to or request restriction of certain types of processing.

You can exercise these rights by:

  • Removing the bot or Mini App from Telegram to stop further processing; and/or
  • Contacting us at satto.stream@proton.me with your request.

We may ask you to verify your Telegram account or identity to help protect your data and prevent misuse of these rights.

8. Children

MarketDesk is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you believe that a child under 18 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at satto.stream@proton.me, and we will take steps to delete such data.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

If the changes are significant, we may also inform you through MarketDesk or related communication channels. Your continued use of the Service after any changes to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of those changes.
